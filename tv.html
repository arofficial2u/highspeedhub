Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Xfinity TV?

Xfinity TV is the residential cable TV service offered by Xfinity from Comcast. Xfinity X1 brings live TV, On Demand, DVR and your streaming apps all in one place. And, the award-winning Xfinity Voice Remote gives you hands-free control of your TV. For channel lineup and Xfinity TV packages & prices in your area, call at 855-742-0945.

What is Xfinity Stream App?

It is an app for Xfinity TV customers to be able to watch all their cable TV networks and On Demand content on any screen at any time. You can also use the app to schedule DVR recordings while on the go.

What is Xfinity On Demand?

Xfinity On Demand is a library of movies and series available to watch at any time, on any screen. It has thousands of titles for everyone in the family to watch―both free and paid.

How to stream Xfinity on a smart TV?

Xfinity offers the Xfinity Stream app for select models of LG and Samsung smart TVs. You will need to have your Xfinity TV and Internet services installed prior to using the Stream app on your smart TV. Create your LG or Samsung account, and link it to your smart TV before you download the Xfinity Stream app on it. When you launch the app, you will be prompted to register your device with your Xfinity credentials. Once logged in, you can start streaming content on your smart TV.

Does Xfinity offer local channels?

Yes. Xfinity does offer local channels from your area. Check the channel list above to get an idea of the local channels you can get on Xfinity TV.

What is Xfinity X1 DVR?

The Xfinity Digital Video Recorder lets you record six shows simultaneously, and gives you 150 HD hours worth of storage space on the hard drive. You also get access to 150 hours of Cloud DVR per X1 TV box in your home.

How do I pay my Xfinity bill?

How do you order Xfinity Pay-Per-View movies?

Can I watch movies and shows on my favorite network’s website?
