Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can I watch my home with Xfinity when away from home?

Yes. Xfinity Home security systems allow you to stay on top of the happenings at your home even when you are away. You can manage and control your system, as well as monitor activity via the Xfinity Home app or the Xfinity app at anytime from anywhere.

How much does Xfinity charge for security?

Xfinity Home offers Home Pro Protection Plus plan for $65.00. Call at 844-207-8721 to learn more.

How does Xfinity Home security work?

With Xfinity Home you pick a monitoring plan and pair it with an Xfinity Home system best for the size of your home. For instance you can subscribe to Pro Protection Plus, and pick the Complete Home system for your town home.

Once the installation is complete all devices are wirelessly connected to the central hub via the xFi Gateway. You can use the touchscreen panel or the Xfinity Home app to manage, control and monitor your security system at anytime from anywhere.

How much does Xfinity Home Cost?

Xfinity Home costs start at $10/month for the Self-Protection plan (DIY monitoring with Xfinity Internet) or about $45–$65/month for professional monitoring, plus a one-time equipment cost of $360–$600 (or $15–$25/month over 24 months) and possible installation fees.
