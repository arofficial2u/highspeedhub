Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What are the benefits of bundling my Xfinity services?

When you sign up for an Xfinity bundle you can benefit from various discounts depending on the terms and conditions of offers available at the time. For instance, if there is a term contract, you can earn a discount for the length of it. There may also be a chance to save with a promotional discount. And then there is the multi-product discount. Besides savings, there is convenience since you get to deal with only one vendor and pay for all services on one bill.

What happens if I remove a service from my Xfinity bundle?

When you remove a service from your Xfinity bundle, the discounts are no longer valid, and you’re charged at a new rate from thereon.

Do Xfinity bundles have hidden fees?

Xfinity like other service providers charges you for installation and equipment, and you also have to pay for the taxes and fees such as the broadcast or regional sports fees. The price you see is exclusive of such extras that can only be determined based on your location and once you complete your order. It is a good practice to read through the fine print that you see displayed with the price. And also ask questions when you are placing an order.

How much does Xfinity charge for internet and TV equipment?

Xfinity xFi Gateway device comes included with its plans, which is a wireless modem + router and provides powerful WiFi coverage around your house. The X1 TV box fee can be up to $12/mo. depending on the region you are in.

What is the cost of Xfinity service installation?

Xfinity charges a one-time $99.99 pro install fee for your internet and TV connection. This fee covers connecting your equipment to the Xfinity network, and also preparing your home for Xfinity services if you have not used the service provider in the past. However, the provider also offers you the choice to self install the service, and set up the equipment, at little to no cost to you. In that case however, you do not remain eligible for no-cost upgrades or tech support.
